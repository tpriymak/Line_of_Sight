Our Services

Driving with a cracked or broken windshield, side glass, or back window is very distracting and can even be dangerous. That is why we at Line of Sight offer a wide range of services to help you get back on the road. Sometimes the damage is not worth the replacement of the whole windshield. If a rock chip is small enough, we can do a simple rock chip repair and we are done. If not, we will replace the windshield on any car, even rare models. Side and back windows are also avaliable if those need to be replaced. We are entirely mobile and will come to your workplace, apartment, house, or any location convenient for you.
